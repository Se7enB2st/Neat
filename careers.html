CDL Operator, Dedicated Runs OTR

Full-Time

Locations:
Pennsylvania – Carnegie/Cecil
DESCRIPTION:

Weavertown Environmental Group is hiring for an Over The Road CDL operator holding a class A CDL with tanker and hazmat endorsement. This position will be hauling a carbon product on dedicated runs from our facility here in Pittsburgh, to the client, and back with home time and rest between runs. All candidates must have 2-3 years of experience and posses both Tanker and Hazmat endorsements along with a clean MVR record.

Compensation for this position will be split between a mileage rate while driving and an hourly rate while performing on-load and off-load of material. Full benefits will include, Medical/Dental/Vision, PTO, 401K, professionally laundered uniform service, company paid training, and hiring bonus.
