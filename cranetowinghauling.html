Safety

Safety is our number one priority. All Weavertown drivers are trained and certified through national training centers such as Towing & Recovery Association of America (TRAA) and Wreckmaster. All drivers are subject to criminal background checks and drug and alcohol testing. Weavertown is an authorized Freightliner tower and preferred service provider for the Pennsylvania State Police, various local law enforcement and government agencies.

Towing and Recovery

Weavertown Towing is a 24/7 emergency responder for truck roll-overs or heavy-duty recovery incidents. Our fleet has wreckers and roll backs that can handle small, medium, or heavy duty weight classifications including our 75-Ton Rotator.
